MBA (HA & HM) Curriculum

Course Structure of MBA (HA & HM) Program

The two years of the MBA (HA & HM) program is divided into six terms, each year consisting of three terms. The first year has core courses and a Summer Internship Program. The second year has elective courses and two Course of Independent Study. The Program structure of MBA (HA & HM) entails every student to compulsory complete:

  1. 64.5 core course credits in the first year
  2. 3.0 credits from the 8-10-week long ‘Summer Internship Program (SIP)’ at the end of Term III with a company or an organization from healthcare industry
  3. 6.0 credits from two ‘Course of Independent Study (CIS)’ in the second year: one in a hospital and the other in a healthcare organization 
  4. 42.0 course credits through the choice of his/her electives in the second year

Additionally, students are permitted to:

  1. Audit Courses (Maximum of 3 courses in the second year) by paying the requisite additional fees.
  2. International Student Exchange Program: Merit Based in the second year
  3. National and International Live Projects
